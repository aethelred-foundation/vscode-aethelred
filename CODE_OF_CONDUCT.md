# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in the Aethelred ecosystem a respectful,
safe, and constructive experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size,
visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity or expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and
orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming,
diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Showing empathy and patience toward other people
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the ecosystem and its users
- Taking responsibility for mistakes and learning from them
- Respecting different viewpoints, backgrounds, and levels of experience

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language
- Trolling, personal attacks, or bad-faith arguments
- Publishing private information without permission
- Deliberate disruption of community spaces or review processes
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Foundation maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of
conduct and may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits,
issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with these standards.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies within all Aethelred-managed GitHub repositories,
issue trackers, pull requests, discussions, documentation, and community spaces
when an individual is representing the project.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behavior to [conduct@aethelred.io](mailto:conduct@aethelred.io).
For security issues, use [security@aethelred.io](mailto:security@aethelred.io)
instead of public channels.

All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as possible.
